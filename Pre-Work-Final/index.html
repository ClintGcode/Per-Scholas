About Me

Software Engineer | Audio Engineer | Digital Artist |

Hello World, my name is Clinton Garwood and I'm a Software Developer from the Bronx, New York. The child of two immigrant parents from the island of Jamaica, at a young age, I was exposed to a culture of grand expressions through art forms such as dance, music, film and photography.

Born in the year 1993, I consider my generation the last children of the analog era and the first of the technological revolution. Over the last decade I've watched and engaged as we rapidly became a society increasingly dependent on technology. For better or for worse? I focus on the better, as I use technology to better myself, with hopes of, one day, developing technology that creates a better world for generations to come.
